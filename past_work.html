  • ✦
    Lennard-Jones Parameters for Noble Gas Clusters (GitHub Wiki)

    GitHub wiki detailing work on derivation and implementation of Lennard-Jones parameters of noble gas clusters in TransRot to probe for minimum energy clusters–discussing:

    • Converting Lennard-Jones parameters to the TransRot potential energy equation
    • Adding noble gases to TransRot
    • Optimizations of small noble gas clusters
    • Possible discovery of local minima during annealing

    Published: October 2024
